### 🧠 About Me  
I'm a passionate **Software Engineer** and **IT Support Specialist** based in **Jakarta, Indonesia**.  
Graduated from **SMK Negeri 12 Jakarta (Software Engineering major)**, I’ve been building things on the web ever since — and occasionally fixing servers when they scream for help 😅.

I've worked on various projects:
- 🗂️ A Kanban-based project management system for a port electricity company  
- 🎓 Internal portals, certificate management platforms, and onboarding systems  
- ⚙️ Tech stacks include **Laravel**, **React**, **Livewire**, **Flutter**, and CI/CD automation  

I believe in *learning by doing* and thrive in fast-paced, problem-solving environments.  
People call me the “**flexible developer**” — not because I do yoga (I don’t… yet),  
but because I can jump between **frontend**, **backend**, **server ops**, and **coffee runs ☕**.  

> Thanks for stopping by! Feel free to check out my repos — and if you love clean code, crazy side-projects, or debugging at 2 AM with a smile, I’m your guy 😄
